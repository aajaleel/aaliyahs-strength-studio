Welcome to the Studio!

New year, new habits? Let's hold ourselves accountable!

We're not here for extreme workouts or quick fixes.
These women-only programs are built for beginners, uncertain or enthusiastic, to understand their bodies and how to feel strong within them.
Programs are tailored for different demographics, but the intention is the same: strength and confidence that sustain beyond these 4 weeks.

Certified as a Personal Trainer and Strength & Conditioning Coach, I have thoroughly studied what health and wellness look like for Muslim ladies.
I've trained 120+ women as a Woman's Fitness Ambassador at the University of Toronto, and have developed Group Fitness Courses tailored towards Muslim mothers.

Program Structure

What to expect?

Week 1:
Why We Move

Learn gentle mobility and Pilates to reduce stiffness, improve movement, and build confidence exercising safely.

Week 2:
Nutrition &
Diet Myths

Understand simple nutrition basics while building strength and flexibility through beginner strength training and yoga.

Week 3:
Menstrual Cycles
& Our Energy

Learn how women’s bodies and energy change, paired with core training and low-impact cardio.

Week 4:
Building Habits

Build sustainable fitness habits with accessible HIIT and guided meditation for mind and body.
